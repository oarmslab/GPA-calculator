GPA Calculator

This application calculates the GPA for most higher education institutions in Cameroon. To determine your GPA, first select the number of courses and choose the GPA scale. Then, enter the course titles, the grades earned, and the corresponding credit values. Finally, click the 'Save Subjects' button, and a summary table displaying your results and calculated GPA will be provided.
